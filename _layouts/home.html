Welcome! I'm Arpan

Delighted to have you here! I'm Arpan, a dedicated scientist immersed in the captivating realms of theoretical and computational chemistry/physics. My expertise revolves around advanced functional and quantum materials, fueled by a robust academic foundation and hands-on experience.

I proudly earned my Ph.D. with summa cum laude from Humboldt University of Berlin in theoretical and physical chemistry, marking the inception of a fascinating journey. With over 5 years of post-Ph.D. research, I've delved deep into crafting and employing cutting-edge theoretical and computational tools. These tools unravel the mysteries of materials science, amplifying advancements in quantum technologies, semiconductor innovations, and addressing critical energy and sustainability challenges.

This website is a testament to my commitment to pushing the boundaries of scientific exploration. Explore my CV, publications, research, and software to delve into the intricate tapestry of my professional odyssey.

Thank you for your visit, and I hope you find my journey as thrilling as I've experienced it!
